FAQs – Top Design Strategy Firms

### What does a design strategy firm do?

A design strategy firm aligns product design with business goals using research, product strategy, prototyping, and measurement. They help companies improve conversion, retention, and user experience using structured discovery and strategic planning.

### How do I choose the best design strategy firm for my product?

While choosing, look for relevant case studies, strong research capability, clear KPIs, and a structured discovery-to-measurement process. Domain expertise (SaaS, eCommerce, enterprise) is crucial.

### How much do design strategy services cost?

The services typically cost $15k–$40k for startups, $40k–$120k for mid-market, and $120k–$250k+ for enterprise.

### How long does it take to see results from a design strategy engagement?

Most companies begin seeing improvements in conversion or onboarding within 6–12 weeks after implementation.

### What questions should I ask a design strategy firm during RFP?

As a business owner, you should ask these questions to design strategy firm during RFP:

- Research methods
- Prototyping process
- Domain expertise and case studies
### Should startups hire a design strategy agency or build in-house?

Startups would benefit from agencies early because they get senior strategy talent immediately without hiring overhead.

### What metrics prove a design strategy firm is delivering ROI?

These are the metrics one must look for in a design strategy firm:

- Conversion rate lifts,
- Onboarding completion improvements,
- Reduced churn,
- Faster time-to-first-value, and fewer support tickets.
