### What is custom website design?

It is a website that’s designed exclusively for your brand to meet your specific goals, and to specifically serve your users.

### Why choose a custom website?

Going custom gives businesses all the countless benefits of UI/UX design. Unique branding, better performances, premium UX, easy scalability, and more.

### How much does custom website design cost?

You need $5k-$50k+ to build one from scratch. The cost to redesign a website and make it custom falls within a similar range.

### Is custom website design good for small businesses?

Yes, it is, especially if scaling or standing out is critical.

### How long does it take to create a custom website?

It takes 6-16 weeks. The design’s complexity will dictate the final timeline.
