### What are the UI/UX design project cost?

It ranges from $2k-$50k. The cost to redesign a website will be closer to $2k. Crafting a complex SaaS app takes the range closer to $50k.

### Which factors affect UI/UX design pricing?

The factors include project complexity, the number of screens/features, and the designers’ expertise and location.

### Is UI/UX design worth the cost?

Yes, absolutely. Study the different UI/UX design process steps. You’ll see how each step adds more value than it costs.

### How long do UI/UX design projects take?

It takes 2-4 weeks for a simple website and 12+ weeks for a larger enterprise-grade product.

### What is the hourly rate for UI/UX designers?

The rate is $20-$40/hr in Asia, and $80-$150/hr in the US & Europe.
