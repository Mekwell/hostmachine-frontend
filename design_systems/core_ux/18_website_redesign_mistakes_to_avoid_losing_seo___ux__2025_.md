### Will a redesign hurt my SEO?

It can if you fail to redirect old URLs or delete valuable content.

### What’s the common website redesign mistake companies make?

Changing URL structures without a comprehensive 301 redirect plan is one of the most important mistakes to avoid during redesign

### Can I change URL structures safely?

Yes, but only with a meticulous one-to-one redirect map.

### What should I check right after launch?

Confirm all redirects are working. Check Google Search Console for crawl errors. Manually test all conversion forms and checkout paths.

### How long does SEO recovery take after a redesign?

It depends on the number and types of errors in website redesign. Usually, anywhere from a few weeks to several months.
