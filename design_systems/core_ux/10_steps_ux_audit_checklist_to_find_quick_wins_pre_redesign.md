### What does a UX audit checklist consist of?

A checklist covers all the tasks required to be done before, during, and after a UX audit.

### What is the significance of a UX audit?

It takes an honest, detailed look at how users experience your product. Then, it shows you a clear, fact-based way to make the experience better.

### What are the popularly used UX audit tools?

Google Analytics, Hotjar, UserTesting, Figma, Optimal Workshop, etc.

### How often should a UX audit be performed?

Once in the interval of every 12-18 months

### How does a UX audit differ from usability testing?

Usability testing is a singular method to observe user behaviour.
It is one of the many methods used in UX audits to decode the drivers of user behaviour

### Can a UX audit improve conversion rates?

Yes. By identifying and fixing friction points in user journeys, it can directly address all existing barriers to conversion.

### How long does a UX audit take?

It takes around 1-3 weeks for focused audits. 4-8+ weeks for complex audits.
