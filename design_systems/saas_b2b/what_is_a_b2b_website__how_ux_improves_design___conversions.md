## Quick Summary

B2B website design refers to the process of creating a website that helps a business evaluate the offerings of another brand. It removes friction and confusion from the decision-making process and guides visitors through the stages of their buyer journey.

### What makes a good B2B website?

A good B2B website communicates what the company does, who it serves, and why it matters. It offers structured content for different decision-makers, builds trust with proof points, and provides simple, low-friction paths to convert.

### How much does B2B website design cost?

The cost to design a B2B website may fall anywhere between $5000 and $30,000. It may vary depending on certain factors, such as feature complexity, pages, chosen platform, and additional integrations.

### Is SEO important for B2B websites?

Yes, SEO is important for B2B websites to establish authority and credibility, increase visibility and traffic, build a brand’s lead generation pipeline and boost revenue.

### How long does B2B website design take?

The timeline for B2B website design may vary, from 3-5 weeks to 6+ months. However, a typical B2B website might take 10-15 weeks, from discovery to post-launch optimization.

### What pages should every B2B website have?

A B2B website should have a well-designed Home Page, About Us, Services/ Products, Case Studies, Blog, and legal/ compliance related pages (Privacy Policy, Terms and Conditions), and Return/ Refund Page for B2B e-commerce websites.
