## SaaS Onboarding UX Summary

SaaS onboarding is the guided journey that helps new users understand your product, reach value, and build habits. Strong SaaS Onboarding UX focuses on a clear “Aha!” moment, fast time to value, simple signup, and helpful in-product guidance instead of noisy tours. When you define activation events, measure them, and keep removing friction, onboarding stops being a one-time setup screen and becomes a reliable growth engine.
